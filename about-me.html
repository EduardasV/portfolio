About Me

What are my interests you may ask? To start off, I am really interested in Artificial Intelligence; this topic is fascinating, because my biggest dream is to be able to co-exist with technology not as tools, but something much more grand. For example, having a Robot assisting you with things you can't normally do yourself or even helping you discover new things and fixing problems we still need answers to would be amazing... I believe this is what technology should be, and with artificial intelligence we will be able to achieve this.

Another interest I have is software engineering - compared to Artificial Intelligence you may be asking why software engineering? The reason I am interested in software engineering is because I enjoy seeing a project being made by me from the beginning till the end and participating through every stage; designing; developing and completing. The thrill you get when you finish a project you have been working on from the beginning till the end is something that words can't describe.
